# Others

## Reference

```
> Knowledge is Power
  >> Francis Bacon
  
<cite>Knowledge is Power</cite> --- Francis Bacon
{: .small}
```

> Knowledge is Power
  >> Francis Bacon
<br>
<cite>Knowledge is Power</cite> --- Francis Bacon
{: .small}
