# Others

## Reference

```
> Knowledge is Power
  >> Francis Bacon
  
<cite>Knowledge is Power</cite> --- Francis Bacon
{: .small}
```

> Knowledge is Power
  >> Francis Bacon
<br>
<cite>Knowledge is Power</cite> --- Francis Bacon
{: .small}

## Toggle List

'''

\😎Hidden Message😎

\</div>
\</details>
'''

<details>
<summary><u>Click here</u></summary>
<div markdown="1">       

😎Hidden Message😎

</div>
</details>
